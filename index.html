🙋 About Me

Hey, I'm Logan — a student, music enthusiast, and someone who takes both lifting and friendship pretty seriously. I'm currently studying at BYU figuring out how technology and business fit together, and honestly loving the ride.

🎓 Education

I'm studying Information Systems at Brigham Young University (BYU) in Provo, Utah. Information Systems sits right at the crossroads of technology and business — I get to learn how organizations use tech to solve real problems, which I find way more interesting than pure coding or pure business on its own.

The program covers everything from database design and networking to project management and cybersecurity basics, which keeps things fresh and always gives me something new to dig into.

🎸 Interests

Music is a big part of my life — specifically 90s rock and grunge. There's something about that raw, unfiltered sound that just hits different. Bands like Nirvana, Pearl Jam, Soundgarden, and Alice in Chains are basically a permanent fixture in my rotation. It's the kind of music that doesn't try too hard and doesn't apologize for anything — which I can respect.

Beyond just listening, I love learning about the stories and history behind the music. The Seattle scene of the early 90s is genuinely one of the most fascinating cultural moments in modern music history, and I could talk about it for way too long.

💪 Hobbies

The gym is part of my daily routine — not just for the physical side, but because it's one of the best ways I know to clear my head and stay consistent. There's something satisfying about showing up every single day and putting in the work, even when you don't feel like it.

Outside of that, I spend a lot of time with my friends. Whether it's hanging out, grabbing food, or just vibing — good company is hard to beat. Some of my favorite memories are just low-key hangouts that turned into something unexpectedly great.
